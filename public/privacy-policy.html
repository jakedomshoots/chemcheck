ChemCheck Privacy Policy

TL;DR: ChemCheck stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

None. ChemCheck is designed as a local-first application that stores all data directly on your device using your browser's local storage (IndexedDB).

2. Data Storage

All information you enter into ChemCheck, including:

  • Customer information (names, addresses, contact details)
  • Pool service logs and chemical readings
  • Notes and chemical usage records
  • Application preferences and settings

Is stored exclusively on your local device and never transmitted to external servers.

3. Data Sharing

We don't share your data because we don't have access to it. Since all data remains on your device, there is no data sharing with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

4. Data Security

Your data security depends on:

  • Your device's security settings and access controls
  • Your browser's security features
  • Regular backups you create using the app's export feature

We recommend using the built-in backup feature regularly to protect against data loss.

5. Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you:

  • Manually delete it within the application
  • Clear your browser's storage/cache
  • Uninstall the application

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is always accessible within the app
  • Export: Use the backup feature to export all data
  • Delete: Remove individual records or clear all data
  • Portability: Export and import data between devices

7. Technical Information

ChemCheck may access the following device features (only when explicitly requested):

  • Local Storage: To save your pool service data
  • File System: To export/import backup files (with your permission)

No network requests are made for data storage or synchronization.

8. Children's Privacy

ChemCheck does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed for professional pool service use.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date below. Since the app operates offline, you may need to refresh the app to see policy updates.

10. International Users

Since ChemCheck operates entirely offline with local data storage, there are no international data transfers or jurisdiction-specific considerations.
