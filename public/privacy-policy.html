ChemCheck Privacy Policy

Summary: ChemCheck is a pool service operations platform. We store your business data in secure cloud services and keep a local copy on your device for offline use. We use trusted third-party providers for authentication, billing, and reliability. You can export or delete your data at any time from Settings.

This Privacy Policy describes how ChemCheck ("ChemCheck," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our website, mobile apps, and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account and identity information

When you create an account, we collect information needed to authenticate you and operate your workspace, such as:

  • Name and email address
  • Authentication identifiers and session metadata
  • Business profile details you provide during setup

Authentication is handled by Clerk, our identity provider.

1.2 Business and customer data you enter

You may store operational data in ChemCheck, including:

  • Customer names, addresses, phone numbers, and email addresses
  • Service logs, chemical readings, notes, and route information
  • Photos captured for proof-of-service
  • Chemical usage, invoices, quotes, and work order records
  • Report settings and communication preferences

1.3 Device and local storage data

ChemCheck uses local device storage (IndexedDB and related browser or app storage) to cache data for offline access, improve performance, and queue changes for sync when connectivity returns.

1.4 Payment and subscription information

If you purchase a paid plan, subscription and billing details are processed by Stripe. ChemCheck receives subscription status and limited billing metadata needed to provide the Service. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers.

1.5 Diagnostics, analytics, and support data

We may collect technical and usage information to keep the Service reliable and improve product quality, including:

  • Error reports and performance diagnostics through Sentry
  • Optional, anonymized product analytics if you have not opted out in Settings
  • App version, device type, and basic interaction events needed for troubleshooting

1.6 Communications you send through the Service

If you send customer reports by SMS or email, we process recipient contact details and message content needed to deliver those communications and maintain delivery records.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and secure the Service
  • Authenticate users and enforce account access controls
  • Sync data between your devices and our cloud backend
  • Generate and deliver service reports to your customers
  • Process subscriptions and manage billing status
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Respond to support requests and legal obligations
  • Improve performance, reliability, and product features

3. Where Data Is Stored

ChemCheck uses a hybrid storage model:

  • Cloud database: Team-shared business data is stored in Convex, our backend platform.
  • Local cache: A copy of relevant data is stored on your device for offline-first operation.
  • File storage: Service photos and related media may be stored in cloud object storage associated with your account.

Data remains available locally for a period after sign-out, but cloud-backed features require an authenticated account and network connectivity.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate the Service:

Provider Purpose Data involved
Clerk Authentication and account management Email, name, auth/session identifiers
Convex Cloud database and application backend Business, customer, and service data you store in ChemCheck
Stripe Subscription billing Billing contact details, subscription status, payment metadata
Sentry Error monitoring and diagnostics Error events, device/browser metadata, limited user context
Email/SMS delivery providers Customer report delivery Recipient contact details and report content you choose to send

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale with appropriate safeguards.

5. Device Permissions

Depending on how you use ChemCheck, the app may request access to:

  • Camera and photo library: To capture and attach proof-of-service photos
  • Local storage: To save data offline and improve sync reliability
  • Notifications: To deliver optional service reminders if you enable them
  • Network access: To authenticate, sync data, send reports, and process billing

You can deny permissions in your device settings, but some features may not work without them.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you delete your account, we delete or de-identify associated cloud account data within a reasonable period, subject to:

  • Backup retention cycles
  • Legal, tax, or fraud-prevention obligations
  • Records required to resolve disputes or enforce agreements

Local device data may remain until you clear app storage, uninstall the app, or use in-app deletion tools in Settings.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, restrict, or delete personal information. ChemCheck provides self-service tools in Settings > Privacy & Data, including:

  • Export your data: Download a machine-readable copy of your records
  • Delete all data: Remove local records from the current device
  • Delete account: Permanently delete your account and associated cloud data
  • Analytics opt-out: Disable optional usage analytics in Settings

To make a privacy request, contact us using the information below. We may need to verify your identity before completing certain requests.

8. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information, including encrypted transport (HTTPS), access controls, and monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we also recommend regular exports using the built-in backup tools.

9. Children's Privacy

ChemCheck is intended for business use by pool service professionals and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International Users

If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. Those locations may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date below and, where appropriate, provide additional notice in the app or by email.
